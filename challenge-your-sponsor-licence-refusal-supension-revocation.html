Challenging Your
Sponsor Licence Decision

A suspension or revocation does not have to be the end. We combine expert legal challenges with rapid compliance reconstruction to fight for your business.

Book Confidential Consultation How We Fight Back

Do Not Accept the Decision Without Review

Many Home Office decisions are overturned when challenged correctly. Navigating this difficult period requires a dual strategy: legal force and compliance proof. IRS HR provides both.

Procedural Errors

We often find the Home Office has failed to follow their own guidance during the revocation process.

Time-Critical Action

You have a limited window to challenge. Immediate intervention is required to pause enforcement.

How We Support Your Restoration

Our specialised panel of immigration solicitors works in tandem with our compliance experts to build a bulletproof case.

1. Legal Challenge

Our solicitors conduct a forensic review of your case to identify procedural errors and grounds for appeal in the Home Office’s decision letter.

2. Judicial Review

We provide the heavy lifting required for Pre-Action Protocols (PAPs) and Judicial Reviews to fight for your licence reinstatement in court if necessary.

3. Compliance Reconstruction

While the legal team fights, IRS HR rebuilds your infrastructure. We create a "Golden Record" to prove to the Home Office you are "Audit Ready" for immediate reinstatement.
