Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can. IRS HR is designed for flexibility. Depending on your business needs, you can upgrade, downgrade, or modify your plan.

Do I need to commit long-term?

Not at all. IRS HR is a subscription-based cloud service with no long-term contracts or hefty upfront costs. Use it as long as it works for you, cancel anytime.

Do you offer annual subscription plans?

Yes. We offer annual billing options based on your current plan and number of users. If your team size or plan changes during the year, your billing will be updated accordingly.

Is there a cancellation fee?

No. There are no cancellation charges, and you can stop your subscription at any time. Note: For annual subscriptions, any remaining balance is non-refundable upon early cancellation.

IRS HR is an all-in-one subscription that allows you to manage your HR and your sponsor compliance needs at just one place. We are also the most affordable, compliant, and easy-to-use HR sponsor compliance platform. Switching to IRS HR is smooth and hassle-free. Most transitions are completed within a week, without disrupting your operations.

We implement:

  • Strong password and login policies
  • Complete data isolation between accounts
Our infrastructure is built with compliance in mind, helping you confidently meet regulatory requirements like UKVI, GDPR, and data retention mandates.

You do, 100%. All data you upload to IRS HR belongs to you. You can access, download, or export your data at any time, without restrictions or hidden conditions. We believe in full transparency and data autonomy.
