Three Reasons Your Current HR Sponsor Systems are unprepared for the Home Office

The "Unpaid Leave" Trap

A worker takes unpaid leave. HMRC sees a drop in pay. If your SMS report isn't filed within 10 days, the system flags it as an illegal underpayment. The Home Office is checking the records of all sponsored workers since 2021.

The PAYE Code Mismatch

You assigned a SOC code on the CoS, but payroll used a generic description. The automated system sees a mismatch in job roles. Audit triggered.

The Timing Gap

You updated the salary in payroll but forgot the SMS. Or vice versa. Even a one-month discrepancy creates a permanent "Compliance Stain" on your record.
