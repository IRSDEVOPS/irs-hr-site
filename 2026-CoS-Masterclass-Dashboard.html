Securing the CoS:
The Vacancy Mathematics

The Home Office no longer accepts "hiring intentions." You must prove a Genuine Vacancy using mathematical certainty. This is the evidence that wins.

  • ● Gather: Total guaranteed hours in your client/service contracts.
  • ● Deduct: Current total hours worked by your existing staff.
  • ● Result: Provable Vacant Hours - the undisputed figure for your CoS request.
