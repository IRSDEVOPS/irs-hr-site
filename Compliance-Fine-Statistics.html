2. June 2026 Revocation Guidance Update

Alongside financial penalties, the Home Office has redrafted its core sponsor guidance to clarify exactly when a sponsor licence will be revoked.

The "Not the Direct Employer" Warning

A critical update in Part 3 of the Home Office guidance issues a stark warning: your duties apply even if you aren't the direct employer.

"Where the statutory right to work scheme does not apply (for example, where you are not the worker’s direct employer) but you are otherwise sponsoring a worker who does not have the relevant permission to work, we are likely to revoke your licence."
