How to use this calculator

  • Match your Rota: Toggle the work days (M-S) to match the employee's standard contract. For care staff, this might include weekends.
  • Hours vs Pay: Adjust the Weekly Hours first. The hourly rate will automatically update if you change the annual salary.
  • Compliance Check: Ensure the Actual Hours in longer months don't cause the effective hourly rate to fall below the UK National Minimum Wage.
