Helping You Build, Grow, and Thrive

IRS HR is an advanced HR compliance platform dedicated to simplifying workforce management for UK Sponsor Licence holders. We ensure businesses stay compliant with UKVI sponsor licence HR rules through automated visa checks, right-to-work verification, and real-time migrant monitoring. Our goal is to free businesses from sponsor licence HR complexities so they can focus on growth and contribute meaningfully to their communities.
