Home Office Rules Trace

UKVI enforces a rigorous regulatory environment for Tier 2 / Skilled Worker and GBM license holders. Negligence in reporting dates, recouping skills charge, or password sharing under Part 3 constitute grounds for immediate licence revocation.

Appendix D Mandate

Sponsors must keep detailed record packages for all workers. Documents can be paper or digital, but must be easily retrievable upon request. Absences, up-to-date address logs, and payslips must be catalogued meticulously.

Sponsor B-Rating Fee

If downgraded, sponsors must pay a mandatory action plan fee of £1,476. Corrective measures must be finalized within 3 months, or you will face automatic sponsor licence revocation and subsequent worker leave cancellation.
